What is MultiCash...

Save

Earn up to 2.50% APY interest on your digital currency holdings. That is 41.66x the average national savings account rate of 0.06% APY.

Spend

Easily pay for goods and services in a few seconds with no delays. Send and receive MultiCash from anywhere around the world.

No Hidden Fees

You don’t have to worry about account maintenance fees, or random hidden bank charges. The only thing you will see are transaction fees which are as a set 0.50%, just enough to ensure payments are delivered instantly.
